## How It Works

Your recurring expenses are organized into two main areas:

### The Rollup Zone

The rollup combines smaller recurring charges into a single Monarch category. This is useful for:

- Streaming subscriptions (Netflix, Spotify, etc.)
- Small monthly fees
- Any expenses you want to budget together

### Individual Items

Expenses tracked individually, each with their own Monarch category. Use this for:

- Major bills (rent, utilities)
- Expenses you want to track separately
- Items with variable amounts

## Managing Existing Items

Select any item to:

- Edit its details
- Change the amount or frequency
- Move it between rollup and individual tracking
- Delete it from tracking
